## TAB 1: Remove All Sitemaps

**You'll see a list of sitemaps. For each one:**

1. Click the **⋮** (three vertical dots) on the right
2. Click **"Remove sitemap"**
3. Click **"OK"**

**Repeat until the list is EMPTY**

---

## TAB 1: Submit Fresh Sitemap

1. In the text box at top, type: `sitemap.xml`
2. Click **"Submit"**

✅ **Done with sitemaps**

---

## TAB 2: Remove Old URLs

1. Click **"New Request"** button
2. Select **"Temporarily remove URL"**
3. Enter: `https://elevate-lms-git-main-selfish2.vercel.app`
4. Click **"Next"**
5. Select **"Remove all URLs with this prefix"**
6. Click **"Submit Request"**

**Repeat for:**
- `https://elevate-lms.vercel.app`
- Any other `.vercel.app` URLs you see

✅ **Done with removals**
