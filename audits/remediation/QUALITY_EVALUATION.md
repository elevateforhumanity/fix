## Evaluation Criteria

Each page is evaluated on 5 dimensions:
1. **Content** - Real copy vs placeholder
2. **Images** - Quality, relevance, optimization
3. **CTA** - Correct action for page purpose
4. **Flow** - Logical navigation paths
5. **Consistency** - Matches site patterns

### Rating Scale
- ✅ PRODUCTION-READY - Meets all criteria
- ⚠️ NEEDS FIX - Has specific issues to address
- ❌ UNACCEPTABLE - Stub, broken, or misleading
