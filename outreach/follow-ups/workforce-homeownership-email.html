I wanted to follow up by sharing a formal collaboration proposal that outlines a structured pathway we have developed specifically for housing authority partners and workforce agencies — the Workforce to Homeownership Mobility Program.

Workforce Training → Paid Employment → Wage Growth → Housing Stability → Homeownership Readiness

Participants do not simply enroll in classes. They enter a defined progression model that includes structured preparation, industry-aligned training, paid workforce engagement, and transition toward financial independence.

Program Stages

1. Workforce Readiness & Career Alignment
Participants complete intake and readiness screening, career interest mapping, barrier assessment, and development of an Individual Workforce Plan — ensuring accountability and commitment before entering technical instruction.

2. Workforce Preparation
Modules covering professional communication, attendance and accountability standards, workplace expectations, financial literacy planning, and an introduction to the housing-to-homeownership pathway.

3. Industry Training & Credentialing
Competency-based, employer-aligned training in Healthcare, Skilled Trades (including HVAC), and Technology/Business Support. Programs are approved on Indiana’s Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL) through WorkOne. Eligible participants access training at no out-of-pocket cost through WIOA and Workforce Ready Grant funding.

4. Employer Alignment & Paid OJT
Elevate coordinates employer interviews, paid On-the-Job Training placements, and direct hire employment pipelines. Participants enter the workplace credential-ready — improving retention, safety, and long-term workforce stability.

5. Income Stabilization & Housing-to-Homeownership Pathway
Post-placement support includes wage progression monitoring, financial coaching referrals, credit-building support, savings planning, and structured transition toward homeownership readiness.
