I wanted to follow up by sharing a formal collaboration proposal that outlines a structured pathway we have developed specifically for housing authority partners and workforce agencies — the **Workforce to Homeownership Mobility Program**.

This program is designed to move residents through a complete economic mobility pathway:

**Workforce Training → Paid Employment → Wage Growth → Housing Stability → Homeownership Readiness**

The model includes five structured phases:

**1. Resident Identification & Intake**
Participants complete a structured intake process including a work readiness evaluation, career interest mapping, barrier assessment, and development of an Individual Workforce Plan.

**2. Workforce Preparation**
Participants complete preparation modules covering professional communication, attendance and accountability standards, workplace expectations, financial literacy planning, and an introduction to the housing-to-homeownership pathway.

**3. Technical Training & Credentialing**
Participants engage in employer-aligned training in Healthcare, Skilled Trades (including HVAC), and Technology/Business Support. Our programs are competency-based, credential-focused, and approved on Indiana's Eligible Training Provider List (ETPL) through WorkOne. Proctored credential testing is integrated to ensure recognized certification completion. Eligible participants access training at no out-of-pocket cost through WIOA and Workforce Ready Grant funding.

**4. Employer Alignment & Paid OJT**
Elevate coordinates employer interviews, paid On-the-Job Training placements, and direct hire employment pipelines. Participants enter the workplace with baseline credentials already in hand, which improves retention, safety, and long-term workforce stability.

**5. Employment Stabilization & Housing-to-Homeownership Pathway**
Post-placement support includes wage progression monitoring, financial coaching referrals, credit-building support, savings planning, and transition toward homeownership readiness programming — moving residents from employment into long-term economic mobility.

---

**Outcomes We Track**

Elevate measures accountability through:
- Credential attainment rates
- Employment placement rates
- 90-day retention
- Wage progression
- OJT placement rates
- Housing stability milestones

---

**Federal Policy Alignment**

This program is designed in alignment with national policy trends, including the One Big Beautiful Bill Act, which reflects a broader commitment to expanding workforce participation and structured employment pathways that build long-term earning capacity and housing independence.

Our programs are aligned with WIOA, Workforce Ready Grant (WRG), and Justice Reinvestment Initiative (JRI) funding frameworks. Elevate for Humanity holds 501(c)(3) nonprofit status, government vendor and state bidder status, and approved proctored testing capability.
