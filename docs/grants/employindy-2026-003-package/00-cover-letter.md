RE: Proposal Submission — RFP 2026-003, WIOA In-School Youth Service Provision

Dear Ms. Pamer and the EmployIndy Review Committee,

Elevate for Humanity respectfully submits this proposal in response to RFP 2026-003 for WIOA In-School Youth Service Provision. We are a Marion County nonprofit workforce development organization with an established DOL Registered Apprenticeship Program, a proprietary AI-enabled Learning Management System, and an active partnership with Warren Central High School (MSD Warren Township) to deliver career coaching and pre-apprenticeship services to WIOA-eligible youth ages 16–19.

Elevate for Humanity is uniquely positioned to deliver this contract because:

1. We are an existing EmployIndy and WorkOne partner — this is not a new relationship.
2. We hold active DOL Registered Apprenticeship Program status (Provider ID 206251) for the Building Services Technician occupation, providing a direct, federally recognized credential pathway (EPA 608 Universal Certification) for every participant we serve.
3. Our proprietary LMS uses AI-assisted content delivery, automated progress tracking, and real-time dashboard reporting — directly aligned with EmployIndy's stated goal of technology-enabled, data-informed service delivery.
4. We project exceeding all five WIOA Youth performance targets, including a 75% credential attainment rate (vs. 68% target), driven by EPA 608 as a built-in credential pathway.
5. Elevate for Humanity is a woman-owned, Black-led organization, IMM certified, serving the same communities that Warren Central High School serves.

This package contains the following documents:

1. Cover Letter (this document)
2. Proposal Narrative — all 8 sections
3. WIOA 14 Service Elements Plan
4. Projected Performance Outcomes
5. Budget & Budget Narrative
6. MOU — Elevate for Humanity & Warren Central High School
7. Organizational Chart

We are committed to the success of every participant we serve and to the long-term workforce development of Marion County. We welcome the opportunity to present to the EmployIndy review committee and are available at any time for questions or clarification.
