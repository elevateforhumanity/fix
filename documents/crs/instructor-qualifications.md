**Program:** Certified Recovery Specialist (CRS)
**Provider:** Elevate for Humanity Career & Training Institute
**Partner Organization:** Selfish Inc. (501(c)(3))
**Credential Authority:** Indiana DMHA / INARR
**Document Purpose:** Submitted in support of CRS training provider approval and fee waiver request

---

## Minimum Instructor Qualifications

All instructors delivering CRS training through Elevate for Humanity must meet the following baseline requirements before leading any module.

### Required

| Requirement | Detail |
|-------------|--------|
| Active Indiana CRS or CPRS credential | Current, in good standing with DMHA |
| Minimum 2 years direct recovery support experience | Post-credential, documented |
| Personal lived experience with recovery | Self-identified; not required to disclose specifics |
| Background check clearance | Indiana state criminal history check, completed within 12 months |
| Mandatory reporter training | Completed within 24 months |
| HIPAA/42 CFR Part 2 training | Completed within 24 months |

### Preferred (not required for approval)

- Bachelor's degree in social work, counseling, psychology, or related field
- Experience with justice-involved populations
- Familiarity with Indiana WIOA and Workforce Ready Grant processes
- Prior training facilitation or adult education experience

## Instructor Onboarding Process

Before any instructor delivers content:

1. Submit credential verification to program director (copy of active CRS/CPRS card)
2. Complete background check (Indiana state, within 12 months)
3. Complete HIPAA/42 CFR Part 2 refresher (if not completed within 24 months)
4. Review CRS curriculum outline and module learning objectives
5. Complete one observed practice delivery with program director feedback
6. Sign instructor agreement (scope of role, confidentiality, mandatory reporting obligations)

---

## Continuing Education Requirements

All instructors must maintain their CRS/CPRS credential in good standing throughout the program delivery period. Credential lapses require immediate notification to the program director and temporary suspension from instruction until the credential is reinstated.

---

## Documentation Retained on File

The following records are maintained by Elevate for Humanity for each instructor:

- Copy of active CRS/CPRS credential
- Background check clearance letter
- HIPAA/42 CFR Part 2 training certificate
- Mandatory reporter training certificate
- Signed instructor agreement
- Observation/feedback record from practice delivery

Records are available for DMHA/INARR review upon request.
