# CRS Training Program — Partner and Employer Pipeline

**Program:** Certified Recovery Specialist (CRS)
**Provider:** Elevate for Humanity Career & Training Institute
**Partner Organization:** Selfish Inc. (501(c)(3))
**Credential Authority:** Indiana DMHA / INARR
**Document Purpose:** Submitted in support of CRS training provider approval and fee waiver request

---

## Purpose

This document describes the partner and employer pipeline that connects CRS graduates to employment and work-based learning opportunities. Indiana DMHA and INARR require training providers to demonstrate a clear pathway from credential to employment. This pipeline identifies target referral partners and employers currently being engaged through active outreach.

## Work-Based Learning Pathway

For learners who complete the CRS training but need additional experience before direct employment, Elevate for Humanity offers a practicum placement pathway.

### Practicum Placement

The program is delivered in a hybrid format. Didactic instruction is completed online through the Elevate for Humanity platform (Modules 1–7). Hands-on field training is completed at employer partner sites (Module 8).

- Minimum 8 supervised field hours at a partner employer site
- Field training sites are being recruited through active partner outreach
- Confirmed site: Selfish Inc.
- Practicum supervisor at each site must hold active CRS, CPRS, or LCSW credential
- Completion documented in Elevate for Humanity LMS and signed off by site supervisor

Elevate for Humanity is structured to connect learners to these funding streams at enrollment. Funding eligibility is assessed per learner at the point of application.

## Employer Engagement Process

### For New Employer Partners

1. Initial outreach via email or referral from existing partner
2. Program overview call (30 min) with Elizabeth Greene or Elevate for Humanity staff
3. Employer completes a one-page partner agreement (scope, role types, hiring timeline)
4. Employer listed in Elevate for Humanity employer directory (platform)
5. Employer receives graduate profiles 30 days before cohort completion
6. Placement tracked in Elevate for Humanity platform (employer portal)

### For Existing Partners

- Quarterly check-in call with program director
- Updated graduate availability list sent 30 days before each cohort completion
- Placement outcomes reported to DMHA/INARR as part of annual program report

## Outcome Tracking

Elevate for Humanity tracks the following outcomes for each CRS cohort:

| Metric | Target | Tracking Method |
|--------|--------|----------------|
| Completion rate | ≥ 75% | LMS completion records |
| Credential attainment | ≥ 70% of completers | DMHA credential verification |
| Employment placement (90 days post-completion) | ≥ 60% | Employer confirmation + learner self-report |
| Wage at placement | ≥ Indiana minimum wage | Employer confirmation |
| Retention at 6 months | ≥ 50% | Employer confirmation + learner self-report |

Outcome data is reported to DMHA/INARR annually and available upon request.
